Use our crowd for shipping!

TraDel is a non-commercial Crowd Shipping platform connecting travelers who have some luggage space left to people who search for an affordable shipping option around the globe.

TraDel - Travel Delivery - is the solution for expensive or sometimes unavailable shipping options from commercial providers.




How does it work?

As a sender of a package or traveler delivering it you can post a delivery offer or request on the website or search for it via our search function. Once you found a match, you can privately discuss the shipping conditions.

You are the boss!
TraDel is completely free of charge and you can set your own price and conditions.





What are the benefits?

Sending and delivering with TraDel has multiple benefits:


  • 💸 Affordable shipping: Sending would be much cheaper than traditional shipping options. You can directly negotiate your own delivery fee.

  • 🔀 Flexible shipping: Sending with private travelers offers expanded and personalized shipping options to any location someone is traveling to.

  • 💚 Green shipping: Combining travel and delivery routes can reduce emissions, pollution and noise.

  • 👍 Do good: Make somebodies day by helping delivering packages to their loved ones.

  • 🤑 Subsidize travel costs: Although it's commercial you can make extra money and thus, compensate some of the travel costs.

  • 💡 Travel inspiration: No idea where to travel next? Why don't you choose a requested destination and explore places you never though about?

For further questions please have a look at the FAQs or feel free to contact us.

You are looking for a shipping option or plan to travel?

If you want to send a package or plan to travel and have extra luggage space left, please first register using our registration form.




After registration, you can just post a delivery request or travel offer using the shipping form.
Please first select if you are looking for a shipping option or if you are offering to deliver, then choose the starting point and destination and your date or period for the delivery.
Afterwards, please give information on the package (e.g. approximate size and weight) or the luggage space you have to offer.
Login and your post is ready to go!






You can also have a look at available posts and search for shipping options in our search box.

FAQ

Whats TraDel?
TraDel is a non-commercial crowd shipping platform and was build in a project from the TechLabs Digital Shaper Program organized by TechLabs Dortmund, Germany in the spirit of Tech for Good.

How do I send packages?
Please register and login to the website. Then you are able to post a delivery request with information on destination, preferred delivery time, package weight and size. Also, you can search for available offers. Once you find a traveler you discuss all shipping details directly with them.

How do I offer a delivery?
Please register and login to the website. Then you are able to post a travel offer with information on the journey, travel time as well as your opportunities to carry packages (weight and size maximum). Once you find a sender you discuss all shipping details directly with them.

How do I find existing offers or requests for shipping?
You can check available delivery requests or travel offers under "Delivery Options" Also, you can post an delivery request or travel offer yourself.

Can I cancel my offer or request?
As long as you did not agree with a sender or a traveler on a shipping you can cancel your offers and requests at any time. If you have to cancel it while already discussing a shipment with a sender/traveler please communicate this as soon as possible.

How do we manage payment?
Detailed terms of shipping including the price and payment option are decided by the sender and traveler. You can use different payment options such as bank transfer or PayPal.

How much does the shipping cost?
TraDel does not dictate the shipping cost nor charge any fees. The shipping price is decided by the sender and traveler. However, keep in mind additional costs such as custom fees.

What happens if the package is lost or damaged?
Please carefully pack and secure any fragile item and communicate this to the sender before shipping. As traveler, please directly contact the sending and receiving person if there is any problem while delivering the package. TraDel does not provide any warranty or insurance for shipping.

How to we manage customs?
The sender is responsible to get all information needed for the respective destination country and to fulfill any customs requirements. Please communicate any requirements with the traveler before shipping.

What items are allowed to be shipped?
It is allowed to ship any non-commercial item on which both sender and traveler agrees on. However, there a few prohibited items due to law or security, i.e. items that violate national or international law or items that violate do not fullfill requirements of the transport companies.

What happens with my personal data?
The website only stores user information for usage of the website. The website does not use or process these or additional data for any other purpose. Please make sure that your informations are correct and updated. Furthermore, it is prohibited to use other persons identities for transaction or to share other user information with third parties except for transportation or shipping purpose.
